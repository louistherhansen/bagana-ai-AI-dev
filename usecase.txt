BAGANA AI is an AI-powered platform featuring a chat-based assistant built on the AAMAD Framework to help users create structured PRD and MRD efficiently. Through guided conversations, the assistant supports problem definition, product and market requirements, and strategic alignment, while all outputs are presented in a user-friendly PRD/MRD reporting dashboard. The platform is designed with a simple, intuitive UI/UX, enabling founders, product teams, and business users to easily create, review, and share product documents without technical complexity.
